My Story

ForeverFold Creations started from a simple passion — I love making people feel seen, celebrated, and loved through thoughtful gifts. What began as creating hampers for friends and family quickly grew into something bigger when I realised how much joy a beautifully handcrafted gift could bring.

I'm based in Auckland, New Zealand, and every single item I create is made by hand — no factories, no shortcuts. From sourcing the right products to wrapping every ribbon, I pour real attention and love into every order.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, welcoming a new baby, marking Raksha Bandhan, Mother's Day, Diwali, or simply wanting to say "I'm thinking of you" — I'll help you create a gift that truly means something.

What I Offer

Luxury gift hampers, handcrafted soy wax candle bouquets, baby gifts, and fully personalised gift boxes. Every order can be customised — just reach out and tell me your idea.
